# Disability Insurance Underwriting Guidelines
## Sterling Insurance Disability Coverage Standards

### Overview
Disability insurance provides income protection when policyholders cannot work due to illness or injury. These guidelines ensure appropriate risk assessment and coverage determination for both short-term and long-term disability policies.

---

## Policy Types & Coverage

### Short-Term Disability (STD)
- **Benefit Period**: 3, 6, 12, or 24 months
- **Elimination Period**: 0, 7, 14, 30, or 90 days
- **Benefit Amount**: 60-70% of pre-disability income
- **Maximum Monthly Benefit**: $15,000
- **Age Limits**: 18-65 years

### Long-Term Disability (LTD)
- **Benefit Period**: 2 years, 5 years, 10 years, to age 65, to age 67
- **Elimination Period**: 90, 180, 365, or 730 days
- **Benefit Amount**: 60-80% of pre-disability income
- **Maximum Monthly Benefit**: $30,000
- **Age Limits**: 18-60 years (issue age)

---

## Occupation Classifications

### Class 1 - Professional/Executive (Lowest Risk)
**Examples**: Accountants, lawyers, architects, executives, consultants
- **STD Premium**: Base rate
- **LTD Premium**: Base rate
- **Maximum Coverage**: 80% of income
- **Own Occupation Definition**: Available

### Class 2 - White Collar/Administrative
**Examples**: Teachers, nurses, sales representatives, office managers
- **STD Premium**: Base rate + 10%
- **LTD Premium**: Base rate + 15%
- **Maximum Coverage**: 70% of income
- **Own Occupation Definition**: Available with restrictions

### Class 3 - Skilled Trades/Technical
**Examples**: Electricians, plumbers, technicians, mechanics
- **STD Premium**: Base rate + 25%
- **LTD Premium**: Base rate + 35%
- **Maximum Coverage**: 65% of income
- **Own Occupation Definition**: Limited availability

### Class 4 - Light Manual Labor
**Examples**: Assembly workers, delivery drivers, retail workers
- **STD Premium**: Base rate + 50%
- **LTD Premium**: Base rate + 75%
- **Maximum Coverage**: 60% of income
- **Own Occupation Definition**: Not available

### Class 5 - Heavy Manual Labor (Highest Risk)
**Examples**: Construction workers, miners, loggers, steelworkers
- **STD Premium**: Base rate + 100%
- **LTD Premium**: Base rate + 150%
- **Maximum Coverage**: 60% of income
- **Own Occupation Definition**: Not available

## Medical Underwriting Requirements

### Simplified Issue (No Medical Exam)
- **Age**: 18-50
- **Coverage**: Up to $3,000/month
- **Occupation**: Class 1-3 only
- **Health Questions**: Basic questionnaire only

### Standard Medical Underwriting
- **Age**: 18-65
- **Coverage**: $3,001-$10,000/month
- **Requirements**: Medical questionnaire, attending physician statement
- **Lab Work**: Ages 50+ or high-risk occupations

### Full Medical Underwriting
- **Coverage**: Above $10,000/month
- **Requirements**: Complete medical exam, lab work, medical records
- **Financial Review**: Tax returns, income verification
- **Additional Tests**: EKG (age 45+), stress test (high-risk cases)

---

## Health Risk Factors

### Automatic Decline Conditions
- Active cancer treatment
- Severe mental health conditions with recent hospitalization
- Substance abuse within 2 years
- Chronic fatigue syndrome or fibromyalgia
- HIV/AIDS
- Pregnancy complications (temporary decline)

### Rated/Excluded Conditions
| Condition | Action | Premium Impact |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **Diabetes (controlled)** | Rate up | +25-50% |
| **Back problems** | Exclusion | Standard rate with back exclusion |
| **Depression (stable)** | Rate up | +15-25% |
| **High blood pressure** | Rate up | +10-20% |
| **Sleep apnea (treated)** | Standard | No impact |

### Preferred Health Credits
- **Non-smoker (5+ years)**: -10% premium
- **Regular exercise**: -5% premium
- **Preventive care compliance**: -5% premium
- **Healthy BMI (18.5-24.9)**: -5% premium

---

## Financial Underwriting

### Income Requirements
- **Minimum Annual Income**: $25,000
- **Maximum Coverage**: 80% of income (combined with other disability insurance)
- **Income Verification Required**: For coverage >$5,000/month
- **Self-Employed**: 2 years tax returns + CPA letter

### Total Disability Coverage Limits
| Annual Income | Maximum Monthly Benefit |
|---------------|------------------------|
| $25,000-$50,000 | 70% of income |
| $50,001-$100,000 | 65% of income |
| $100,001-$200,000 | 60% of income |
| $200,001+ | 50% of income |

---

## Special Provisions & Riders

### Available Riders
- **Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA)**: 3% or 5% annually
- **Future Increase Option**: Increase coverage without medical evidence
- **Return of Premium**: Refund premiums if no claims
- **Residual Benefits**: Partial disability coverage
- **Social Security Rider**: Additional benefits if SSDI is denied

### Business Overhead Expense
- **Coverage Period**: 12, 18, or 24 months maximum
- **Benefit Amount**: Up to $50,000/month
- **Eligible Expenses**: Rent, utilities, staff salaries, loan payments
- **Elimination Period**: 30, 60, or 90 days

---

## Claims Experience & Risk Factors

### High-Risk Professions (Additional Scrutiny)
- Healthcare workers (repetitive stress injuries)
- IT professionals (carpal tunnel, back problems)
- Hair stylists/beauticians (standing, chemical exposure)
- Musicians (repetitive stress, hearing issues)
- Airline pilots (FAA medical requirements)

### Regional Considerations
- **High-Claim States**: California, New York, Florida
- **Low-Claim States**: North Dakota, Wyoming, Vermont
- **Weather-Related Risk**: Hurricane, earthquake, tornado zones
- **Economic Factors**: Local unemployment rates, industry trends

## Decision Guidelines

### Automatic Approval
- **Age**: 18-45
- **Occupation**: Class 1-2
- **Coverage**: <$3,000/month
- **Health**: No adverse history
- **Income**: >10x monthly benefit

### Standard Approval with Conditions
- Medical exam required
- Exclusions for pre-existing conditions
- Rate increases for health/occupation risks
- Reduced benefit periods

### Manual Review Required
- **Age**: >55
- **High-Risk Occupation**: Class 4-5
- **High Coverage**: >$15,000/month
- **Complex Medical History**
- **Multiple Recent Claims** (any insurance type)
