# Disability Insurance Guidelines
*Sterling Insurance Company - 2025 Edition*

## Overview
This document establishes comprehensive guidelines for disability insurance underwriting, focusing on occupation-based risk assessment and income protection strategies while maintaining sensitivity for diverse executive audiences.

---

## **🎯 Coverage Types & Limits**

### **Short-Term Disability (STD)**
- **Maximum Benefit**: $3,000/week
- **Benefit Period**: 13-52 weeks
- **Elimination Period**: 0-14 days
- **Coverage Percentage**: 60-70% of gross income
- **Age Limits**: 18-64 at application

### **Long-Term Disability (LTD)**
- **Maximum Benefit**: $30,000/month
- **Benefit Period**: To age 65, 67, or lifetime
- **Elimination Period**: 90-365 days
- **Coverage Percentage**: 60-80% of gross income
- **Age Limits**: 18-60 at application

### **Business Overhead Expense**
- **Maximum Benefit**: $100,000/month
- **Benefit Period**: 12-24 months
- **Coverage**: Business expenses during disability
- **Elimination Period**: 30-365 days
- **Ideal for**: Business owners and professionals

## **📋 Occupation Classification System**

### **Class 1 (Preferred Professional)**
**Risk Level**: Lowest
**Premium Discount**: 15-20% below standard
**Examples**:
- Certified Public Accountants
- Software Engineers and Developers
- University Professors
- Pharmacists
- Optometrists
- Financial Advisors (office-based)

**Characteristics**:
- Primarily office-based work
- Low physical demands
- Minimal injury risk
- High education requirements
- Stable work environment

### **Class 2 (Professional)**
**Risk Level**: Low
**Premium**: Standard rates
**Examples**:
- Physicians (non-surgical)
- Attorneys
- Engineers (consulting)
- Architects
- Insurance Agents
- Real Estate Brokers

**Characteristics**:
- Mix of office and field work
- Light physical demands
- Low to moderate stress levels
- Professional qualifications required
- Generally stable income

### **Class 3 (Skilled Technical)**
**Risk Level**: Moderate
**Premium Surcharge**: 25-50% above standard
**Examples**:
- Nurses
- Physical Therapists
- Technicians (medical, engineering)
- Computer Repair Specialists
- Skilled Craftspeople
- Restaurant Managers

**Characteristics**:
- Moderate physical demands
- Some repetitive motion risk
- Variable work environments
- Technical training required
- Moderate injury potential

### **Class 4 (Service/Sales)**
**Risk Level**: Moderate-High
**Premium Surcharge**: 50-100% above standard
**Examples**:
- Sales Representatives (travel-heavy)
- Property Managers
- Retail Managers
- Truck Drivers (local)
- Auto Mechanics
- Hairstylists/Beauticians

**Characteristics**:
- Higher physical demands
- Increased injury risk
- Variable income potential
- Customer interaction stress
- Equipment operation requirements

### **Class 5 (High Physical Demand)**
**Risk Level**: High
**Premium Surcharge**: 100-200% above standard
**Examples**:
- Construction Workers
- Factory Workers
- Warehouse Personnel
- Delivery Drivers
- Landscapers
- Restaurant Workers

**Characteristics**:
- Heavy physical labor
- High injury risk
- Exposure to hazardous conditions
- Variable work schedules
- Income volatility

### **Class 6 (Hazardous Occupations)**
**Risk Level**: Highest
**Premium Surcharge**: 200-400% above standard or declined
**Examples**:
- Commercial Fishermen
- Underground Miners
- High-rise Construction Workers
- Professional Athletes
- Stunt Performers
- Explosive Handlers

**Characteristics**:
- Extreme physical demands
- High risk of severe injury
- Dangerous work environments
- Specialized skills required
- Unpredictable income patterns

## **💰 Financial Underwriting**

### **Income Verification Requirements**
#### **Coverage Up to $5,000/month**
- Tax returns (2 years)
- Recent pay stubs (3 months)
- Employment verification letter
- Basic financial statement

#### **Coverage $5,001 - $15,000/month**
- Tax returns (3 years)
- CPA-prepared financial statements
- Profit & loss statements (business owners)
- Bank statements (6 months)
- Employment contract or offer letter

#### **Coverage $15,001 - $30,000/month**
- Comprehensive financial audit
- Business valuations (if applicable)
- Income trend analysis (5 years)
- Detailed expense documentation
- Professional income projections

### **Coverage Limits by Income**
```
Annual Income        Maximum Monthly Benefit
$60,000 - $100,000   $3,000 - $5,000
$100,001 - $200,000  $5,001 - $10,000
$200,001 - $400,000  $10,001 - $20,000
$400,001+            $20,001 - $30,000
```

### **Business Owner Considerations**
- **Income Averaging**: 3-5 year average for variable income
- **Business Structure**: Corporation vs. partnership vs. sole proprietorship
- **Economic Dependency**: Key person vs. replaceable role
- **Business Overhead**: Separate coverage consideration
- **Profit Sharing**: Documentation of consistent distributions

## **🏥 Medical Underwriting**

### **Medical Exam Requirements**
#### **Coverage Up to $10,000/month**
- Health questionnaire only
- No medical exam required (ages 18-50)
- Attending Physician Statement if indicated

#### **Coverage $10,001 - $20,000/month**
- Paramedical exam
- Basic blood and urine tests
- EKG if age ≥ 45 or history indicates

#### **Coverage $20,001 - $30,000/month**
- Physician examination
- Comprehensive blood panel
- Stress test if indicated
- Specialist reports if needed

### **Common Medical Conditions - Risk Assessment**

#### **Musculoskeletal Conditions** (High Impact on Disability Risk)
- **Back Problems (chronic)**: 
  - Desk jobs: Standard to +50% premium
  - Physical jobs: +100% to declined
  - Recent surgery: 2-year postponement

- **Arthritis**:
  - Mild/controlled: Standard to +25%
  - Moderate: +50% to +100%
  - Severe: Individual assessment

- **Previous Injuries**:
  - Work-related: Detailed assessment required
  - Sports-related: Consider current activity level
  - Motor vehicle: Long-term impact evaluation

#### **Mental Health Considerations** (Handled Sensitively)
- **Treated Depression/Anxiety**: 
  - Stable on medication: Standard to +50%
  - Recent episodes: 6-12 month postponement
  - Hospitalization history: Individual assessment

- **Stress-Related Conditions**:
  - Occupation-related stress evaluation
  - Coping mechanisms assessment
  - Treatment compliance review

#### **Cardiovascular Conditions**
- **Hypertension (controlled)**: Standard to +25%
- **High Cholesterol**: Preferred to Standard
- **Previous Heart Events**: Individual assessment
- **Family History**: Considered but not disqualifying

#### **Other Common Conditions**
- **Diabetes (Type 2, controlled)**: +25% to +100%
- **Sleep Apnea (treated)**: Standard to +25%
- **Asthma (controlled)**: Standard rates
- **Cancer (5+ years remission)**: Standard to +50%

## **🚗 Lifestyle & Activities Assessment**

### **Motor Vehicle Record**
- **Clean Record**: No impact
- **Minor Violations**: Standard rates
- **Major Violations**: +25% to +50%
- **DUI/DWI**: +100% or declined
- **Commercial Driving**: Occupation class consideration

### **Recreational Activities**
#### **Low Risk Activities**
- Golf, tennis, swimming, hiking
- Gym workouts, yoga, cycling (casual)
- No premium impact

#### **Moderate Risk Activities**  
- Skiing, rock climbing (with safety equipment)
- Martial arts, competitive sports
- +10% to +25% premium or exclusions

#### **High Risk Activities**
- Mountaineering, skydiving, motorcycle racing
- Professional sports participation
- Individual assessment, exclusions, or declined

### **Travel Considerations**
- **Domestic Business Travel**: No impact
- **International Travel (safe countries)**: No impact
- **High-risk Destinations**: Exclusions may apply
- **Extended Foreign Residence**: Individual assessment

## **⚡ Automated Decision Framework**

### **Instant Approval Criteria**
All conditions must be met:
- Ages 25-55
- Occupation Classes 1-3
- Coverage ≤ 60% of verified income
- No adverse medical history in past 5 years
- Clean motor vehicle record
- Standard benefit periods and elimination periods

### **Fast-Track Approval** (24-48 hours)
- Ages 18-60 with minor health issues
- Occupation Classes 4-5 with standard coverage
- Well-documented income history
- Controlled chronic conditions
- Standard underwriting with minimal restrictions

### **Manual Review Required**
- Ages 60+ (all applications)
- Occupation Class 6 or hazardous duties
- Coverage >70% of income
- Recent major medical events
- Complex business income structures
- High-risk lifestyle activities
- Previous disability claims history
