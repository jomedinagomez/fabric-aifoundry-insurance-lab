# Risk Assessment Matrix & Methodology
## Comprehensive Risk Scoring Framework

### Overview
This document provides the detailed methodology for calculating comprehensive risk scores across all insurance products. Risk scores range from 1 (lowest risk) to 10 (highest risk) and inform pricing, approval decisions, and coverage terms.

---

## Multi-Factor Risk Calculation

### Base Risk Score Formula
```
Total Risk Score = (Age Score × 0.25) + (Occupation Score × 0.30) + (Health Score × 0.25) + (Financial Score × 0.15) + (Lifestyle Score × 0.05)
```

### Risk Score Interpretation
| Score Range | Risk Level | Action Required |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| **1.0-2.5** | Preferred Plus | Automatic approval, best rates |
| **2.6-4.0** | Preferred | Standard approval, competitive rates |
| **4.1-6.0** | Standard Plus | May require conditions, standard rates |
| **6.1-7.5** | Standard | Higher rates, possible exclusions |
| **7.6-8.5** | Substandard | Significant rate increases, limited coverage |
| **8.6-10.0** | High Risk | Manual review, possible decline |

## Age Risk Assessment

### Life Insurance Age Scoring
| Age Range | Base Score | Mortality Factor | Additional Considerations |
|-----------|------------|------------------|---------------------------|
| **0-17** | 1.5 | Very Low | Parental consent required |
| **18-25** | 1.0 | Low | Lifestyle risks higher |
| **26-35** | 1.2 | Low | Prime earning years |
| **36-45** | 2.0 | Moderate | Health monitoring begins |
| **46-55** | 3.5 | Moderate-High | Regular medical exams |
| **56-65** | 5.5 | High | Extensive underwriting |
| **66-75** | 7.0 | Very High | Limited coverage options |
| **76+** | 9.0 | Extreme | Rarely approved |

### Disability Insurance Age Adjustments
- **Peak Years (30-45)**: -0.5 to base age score (stable income)
- **Early Career (22-29)**: +0.3 to base age score (job instability)
- **Pre-Retirement (55+)**: +1.0 to base age score (health decline acceleration)

## Occupation Risk Matrix

### Detailed Occupation Categories

#### Category A: Executive/Professional (Score: 1.0-1.5)
- **Executives**: CEOs, VPs, directors
- **Finance**: Investment bankers, financial advisors, accountants
- **Legal**: Attorneys, judges, legal consultants
- **Technology**: Software engineers, IT managers, data scientists
- **Medical**: Physicians (non-surgical), pharmacists, medical researchers

#### Category B: Administrative/Education (Score: 1.5-2.5)
- **Education**: Teachers, professors, school administrators
- **Healthcare Admin**: Hospital administrators, medical office managers
- **Government**: Civil servants, municipal workers
- **Sales**: Inside sales, account managers, real estate agents
- **Consulting**: Management consultants, business analysts

#### Category C: Technical/Skilled Trades (Score: 2.5-4.0)
- **Healthcare**: Nurses, physical therapists, medical technicians
- **Skilled Trades**: Electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians
- **Technology**: Network administrators, technical support
- **Transportation**: Commercial pilots, air traffic controllers
- **Manufacturing**: Quality control, equipment operators

#### Category D: Service/Manual Labor (Score: 4.0-6.0)
- **Public Safety**: Police officers, firefighters, security guards
- **Service Industry**: Restaurant managers, retail supervisors
- **Skilled Manual**: Mechanics, repair technicians, installers
- **Transportation**: Truck drivers, delivery drivers, taxi drivers
- **Construction**: Foremen, skilled construction workers

#### Category E: High-Risk/Physical (Score: 6.0-8.0)
- **Construction**: Heavy construction, roofing, demolition
- **Industrial**: Factory workers, warehouse operators
- **Transportation**: Heavy equipment operators, crane operators
- **Agriculture**: Farmers, ranchers, agricultural workers
- **Service**: Janitors, landscapers, food service workers

#### Category F: Extreme Risk (Score: 8.0-10.0)
- **Mining**: Underground miners, oil rig workers
- **Military**: Active duty combat personnel
- **Entertainment**: Stunt performers, professional athletes
- **Aviation**: Test pilots, crop dusters, helicopter pilots
- **Hazardous**: Bomb disposal, hazmat handlers, deep sea divers

## Health Risk Scoring

### Medical History Impact
| Condition Category | Score Impact | Notes |
|-------------------|--------------|-------|
| **No Medical History** | 0.0 | Perfect health credit |
| **Minor Conditions** | +0.5 | Allergies, minor surgeries |
| **Controlled Chronic** | +1.0-2.0 | Diabetes, hypertension (well-managed) |
| **Significant History** | +2.0-4.0 | Heart disease, cancer (history) |
| **Active Treatment** | +4.0-6.0 | Current major medical treatment |
| **Terminal/Severe** | +8.0+ | Life-limiting conditions |

| Factor | Score Modifier | Life Insurance Impact | Disability Impact |
|--------|----------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| **Non-smoker (5+ years)** | -0.5 | -15% premium | -10% premium |
| **Occasional smoker** | +1.0 | +50% premium | +25% premium |
| **Regular smoker** | +2.0 | +100% premium | +50% premium |
| **Social drinker** | 0.0 | No impact | No impact |
| **Heavy drinking** | +1.5 | +25% premium | +40% premium |
| **Drug use (past)** | +2.0 | +50% premium | +75% premium |
| **Regular exercise** | -0.3 | -5% premium | -10% premium |
| **Dangerous hobbies** | +1.0-3.0 | Varies by activity | Varies by activity |

|--------|----------------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Northeast** | +0.1 | +0.2 | Higher cost of living, stress |
| **Southeast** | +0.2 | +0.1 | Hurricane risk, health factors |

### Urban vs. Rural Considerations
- **Major Metropolitan**: +0.2 (higher crime, pollution, stress)
- **Suburban**: 0.0 (baseline)
- **Small Town**: -0.1 (lower crime, less stress)
- **Rural/Remote**: +0.3 (limited healthcare access, higher accident risk)

## Special Risk Situations

### Multiple Policy Discounts
- **Life + Disability**: -0.3 total risk score
- **Family Coverage**: -0.2 per additional family member
- **Loyalty Discount**: -0.1 for existing customers >3 years
- **Group Coverage**: -0.5 for employer-sponsored plans

### Red Flag Indicators (Require Manual Review)
- Recent application with another carrier
- Requested coverage >20x income
- Multiple policies from different carriers
- Recent major lifestyle changes
- Inconsistent information across applications
- High-risk hobbies not initially disclosed
